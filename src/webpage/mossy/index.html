Callum "Mossy" Douglas

By Oliver Newport, the free encylopedia

Callum "Mossy" Douglas (born March 14, 2007), better known online as MossyFN, is a English video game phenomenon, "The Apprentice" Star and professional basement dweller. He is mostly known for his Fortnite battle against "Bagsman" (Luke Jordan) whom he got destroyed by . He is also known to be not a champ at "R6S"(Rainbow six siege) and particulary average at "Fortnite" and "Overwatch 2".

Callum Douglas was born on March 14 2007 and is of English descent. He was raised in Surrey ,England and became interested in video games at a young age, particularly "Team Fortress 2". He attended Tomlinscote Secondary School in Surrey, England. During his secondary school years, Mossy received his nickname from his peers via his computerscience class finding out his Epic Games acccount name.He was then only referred to as "Mossy" even by some of the teachers.At this secondary school Mossy went on to be apart of the legendary Fortnite battle against a fellow pupil Luke Jordan (Bagsman) whom destroyed young Mossgrall . This did not setback Mossy he went on to defeat another fellow pupil and average Fortnite Player ManaFN (Harvy T).Mossy Continued his school life unbothered by mere mortals other than when his computerscience teacher MR S said "Mossy I swear you get bullied ".Despite school drama Mossy went on to achieve high grades while actively not participating in sports day or PE. With aspirations to become a doctor, Mossy dedicated much of his time focusing on his studies, leading him to spend all night playing "Fortnite" and "R6S". Whenever he needed a break, Mossy would play on his Computer .He then went on to move onto college to do maths physics and computer science and can still be found lerking at Thorpe Park which he attends every week.

Video Game Career

Mossy has been playing "Fortnite" since 2018 and has achieved numerous titles he has appeared in numerous cash cups sadly not winning any . He has also appeared into "R6S" ranked play but has failed to reach "Champ" unlike his peers such as Oliver Newport . Mossy also dabbled in "Overwatch" and "Overwatch 2" but sadly was distinctly average at it but managed to get into his colleges Overwatch E sports Team.

Short TV Career

Whilst at Tomlinscote Secondary School Mossy was signed up by another pupil (Oliver Newport a handsome devil) to the popular TV show "The Apprentice". Oliver wrote his application as a mere joke, adding Callum had a second personality Mossy . However Oliver failed to realise TVs desperation for content and Mossy was quickly accepted . Oliver went on to send in a photo of Mossy in his school uniform which was then accepted aswell ,baring in mind you had to be 18 to enter.Mossy was then offered an interview in London which he sadly did not attend thus ending his short tv career .
